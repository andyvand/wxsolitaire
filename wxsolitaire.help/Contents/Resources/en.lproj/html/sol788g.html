Setting Game Options

You can use several options when playing Solitaire by choosing the Options command from the Game menu.

You can use Draw options to specify how many cards to turn over in the deck. The Draw One option turns over the top card. The Draw Three option turns over three cards.

Scoring options include Standard and Vegas. You can also turn scoring off.

Display options include the following:

UseTo
Timed GameKeep track of the time elapsed during a game.
Status BarDisplay the status bar at the bottom of the Solitaire window to show your score and time.
Keep ScoreKeep a running total of your score from game to game when using Vegas scoring.
Outline DraggingDisplay an outline of the card being moved, and change the color of the space or card you are moving to (if the move is valid).
