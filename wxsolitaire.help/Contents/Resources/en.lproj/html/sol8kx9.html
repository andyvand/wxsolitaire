Playing Solitaire by Using the Keyboard

If you don’t have a mouse, use the following keys to play Solitaire.

PressTo
TABMove among the deck, suit stacks, and row stacks. TAB selects a stack only if that stack is applicable to the next move.
LEFT and RIGHT ARROWMove to the next stack.
UP and DOWN ARROWMove from card to card within the stack.
ENTER or SPACEBARPick up and release a card or group of cards. ENTER or SPACEBAR also turns over a new card in the deck or a stack, and “turns over” the deck itself.
